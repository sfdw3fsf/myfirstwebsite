Navigation


A normal compass wont work on the Grand Line due to each island generating its own magnetic field.
In order to navigate the Grand Line, one needs a special type of compass known as a Log Pose. The Log Pose works by locking on to the magnetism produced by one island and points toward that island. After reaching said island and staying there for a certain amount of time,
the Log Pose will reset and start locking on to the magnetism of the next island. The time it takes for a Log Pose to set i s different on each island; it may take a few hours, it may take a few days, or even a full year.[16][17]
